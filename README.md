## Project Overview
This project presents a Sales Performance Dashboard created in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts.  
The dashboard provides insights into revenue, profit, regional performance, sales channels, product categories, and salesperson performance to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables  
- Pivot Charts  
- Slicers  
- Excel formulas  

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Net Sales:** $188,491.80  
- **Total Profit:** $40,146.63  
- **Total Orders:** 12  

---

## Business Questions Answered
- Which **region and sales channel** generate the highest revenue?
- Which **product category** is the most profitable?
- Who is the **top-performing salesperson** by profit?
- Which **sales band** contributes the most to total revenue?
- How do **monthly net sales** trend over time?

---

## Dashboard Features
- Net sales by **region and channel**
- Profit analysis by **product category**
- Sales distribution by **sales band** (High / Medium / Low)
- Salesperson performance by **net sales and profit**
- Monthly net sales trend
- Interactive slicers for:
  - Category
  - Channel
  - Region
  - Salesperson
  - Time period

---

## Key Insights
- **East & Online** is the highest revenue-generating region-channel combination
- **Electronics** is the most profitable category
- **Lina** is the top salesperson by profit
- The **High sales band** dominates total revenue
- Monthly sales show noticeable fluctuations, highlighting seasonal trends

---

## Project Purpose
This project demonstrates the ability to:
- Analyze sales and profit data
- Build KPI-driven dashboards
- Identify top-performing regions, categories, and salespeople
- Present insights clearly through Excel dashboards and storytelling
